What is FWI?

The Fire Weather Index (FWI) is a globally recognized standard for estimating the intensity and spread potential of wildfires. It integrates temperature, humidity, wind speed, and rainfall to produce a comprehensive fire danger score. Our predictive system uses advanced machine learning models to forecast FWI levels and support proactive planning.
